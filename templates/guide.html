🛠️ Shop Usage Guide

Simple steps for using the system in the tyre shop.

  1. Login with admin or employee credentials.
  2. Add tyres under the Tyres page with brand, size, name, price, and stock.
  3. Add customers with their name, mobile, vehicle number, and vehicle model.
  4. Create billing from the Billing page by selecting a customer and tyre details.
  5. Generate invoice and print it for the customer.
  6. Check sales from the Sales page to see all transactions.
  7. Use Search to quickly find tyre details or customer records.
Daily routine: add stock → add customer → create bill → print invoice.
